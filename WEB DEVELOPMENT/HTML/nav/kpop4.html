THE RELEASE OF MOST AWAITED SINGLE 'SEVEN' BY JK

BTS fans were pleasantly surprised when Jungkook unexpectedly unveiled the release schedule for his upcoming song titled “Seven,” accompanied by a shirtless photograph of himself. On Saturday evening, the renowned agency BigHit Music, representing BTS, took to Twitter to share a poster that detailed the promotional schedule under the hashtags #JungKook, #JungkookSeven, and #JungKook_Seven. Moreover, it has been reported that the talented actor Han So-hee will be featured in the song “Seven.” For now, Jungkook’s agency HYBE and Han So-hee’s agency have given a statement that it will be difficult for them to confirm whether these two will be seen together in the music video. However, we now only have a few more days for the teaser, and things should become clear then.

To start off, his songs “Still With You” and “My You” were officially released on Monday, setting the tone for what’s to come. ‘ While these songs were available on SoundCloud earlier, they are now available on all streaming platforms. Both the songs, Still with You and My You were quick to climb the popularity charts and had taken the number one spot on iTunes charts in as many as 83 countries, including India.

In an exciting development for BTS fans, Jungkook's new song Seven (ft. Latto) has become the fastest K-pop artist to top US iTunes in just one hour and six minutes after its release.

The song has shattered numerous records, showcasing the immense impact of Jungkook's artistry. It quickly soared to the top of music charts, achieving chart-topping positions in many countries.Its catchy hooks, explicitly alluring lyrics and Jungkook's evergreen vocals have struck a chord with fans, which has boosted his global profile as a talented solo artist.

This remarkable achievement not only marked the first time a Korean pop song claimed the top spot in the UK but also solidified Jungkook as the first Asian act to reach the illustrious milestone.
